What is Helpie?

Helpie is a community of like-minded Steemians focused on growth, engagement, and we are all positive on the future of the Steem platform. You can read about what it is about here.

Initiatives

  • Helpie Trivia - A fun little series of posts that help people learn various things about the platform. They are daily posts in the helpie blog.
  • Helpie Meetings - Monday Hangout where we discuss recent Steem events or initiatives, and Friday Post-Shilling where members share other posts of interest.
  • Helpie Bot - Used to help our members grow or show support to undervalued posts.

Tools

  • All Posts Filtering Tool - A tool by @eonwarped to show entire history of posts, filterable by tag, and where you can see payout information as well as downloading the contents to a zip file.
  • Previous Posts Footer - A tool by @odrau that will auto generate a footer including links to previous posts.
