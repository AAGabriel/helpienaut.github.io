What is Helpie?

Helpie is a community of like-minded Steemians focused on growth, engagement, and we are all positive on the future of the Steem platform.

Initiatives

  • Helpie Curation Posts - Undiscovered posts with quality content that our scouts seek out and feature.
  • Helpie Meetings - Regular weekly meetings, follow the main blog for times.
  • Helpie Bot - Used to help our members grow or show support to undervalued posts.

Tools

  • All Posts Filtering Tool - A tool by @eonwarped to show entire history of posts, filterable by tag, and where you can see payout information as well as downloading the contents to a zip file.
  • Previous Posts Footer - A tool by @odrau that will auto generate a footer including links to previous posts.
